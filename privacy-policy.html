Privacy Policy

Last updated: Fri 26 Dec 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how (“we”, “our”, or “us”) handles information when you use our mobile applications and games (“Apps”).

We respect your privacy. Our Apps are designed to collect as little data as possible.

However, our Apps may use third-party services that automatically collect limited non-personal information, such as:

  • Device type and operating system
  • App version
  • Crash logs and performance data
  • Anonymous usage statistics

1. Third-Party Services

Our Apps may rely on trusted third-party services provided by Apple or other platforms (such as analytics or crash reporting tools). These services may collect data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

We encourage you to review Apple’s App Store privacy practices if you want more details on how system-level data is handled.

2. How We Use Information

Any non-personal data collected is used strictly for:

  • Improving app stability and performance
  • Fixing bugs and crashes
  • Enhancing features and user experience

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your data. We do not share personal information with third parties.

4. Children’s Privacy

Our Apps are suitable for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal data, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

5. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect our Apps and ensure that data is handled securely.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
